STORY

A Fortune 500 CPG company wanted to streamline reporting across the organization. Within a single business unit, 250 reports were identified and more than 95% of those reports were redundant. Fractal delivered an end-to-end solution to extract the data from their Azure data lake, enrich it in using Concordia Nexus (an AI-enabled product mapping solution) and then published it back into Azure. This helped in establishing a standardized reporting toolkit for all product categories and key metrics for enhanced business decisions. Fractals toolkit provided better data for decision-making, saved approximately 4,000 hours per year for one region, and was adopted by 14 additional countries throughout Europe and Asia.
