Overview - End to End Student Lifecycle Management

Our preconfigured Student Lifecycle Management(SLM) solution taps into the power of Salesforce platform to digitize the student journey for you and build a student 360 view for you. Features like powerful workflows, automated task assignments and powerful dashboards help you track student enrolments with a mobile first app. Additionally, you can easily track payments, send class reminders and address student enquiries. Thus, our solution enables and automate the entire student acquisition and onboarding journey for you.
