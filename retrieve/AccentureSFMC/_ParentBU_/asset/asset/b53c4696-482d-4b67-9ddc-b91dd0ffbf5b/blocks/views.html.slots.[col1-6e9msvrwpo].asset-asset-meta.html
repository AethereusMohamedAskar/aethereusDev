BYJU’S Tuition Centers

BYJU’S Tuition Center (BTC) offers world-class hybrid tuitions for students of classes 4 to 10. With the help of BYJU’S Tuition Centers, students can easily obtain tuitions, located in their cities. They can access the best Maths and Science tuitions, as well as tuitions for any other subject for CBSE, ICSE and State boards. There are special batches for students of Class 9 and Class 10. The batches for CBSE, ICSE and State Board tuitions run separately.

BYJU’S Tuition Center has highly qualified and experienced teachers who focus on building the conceptual background of students. Their approach not only helps students to perform well in academics, but also ensures that they crack tough competitive exams, such as JEE, NEET, Olympiads, etc. Teachers pay attention to individual students and keep a track of their performance records. Their personalised teaching style is unique in a way that students begin to enjoy the learning process. They make learning interesting by giving examples from real life, and correlating the topics to the applications of the real world. Moreover, the technology, combined with offline learning helps students to visualise the topic, making it easier for them to understand the concept.

The BYJU’S Tuition Center will propel the student to perform at a higher level. It will help them to score higher marks in the exams, and turn them into lifelong learners. BTC provides a visual learning experience to students under the careful guidance of the teacher. Students who wish to experience a unique combination of online and offline learning are encouraged to visit the BYJU’S Tuition Center.
