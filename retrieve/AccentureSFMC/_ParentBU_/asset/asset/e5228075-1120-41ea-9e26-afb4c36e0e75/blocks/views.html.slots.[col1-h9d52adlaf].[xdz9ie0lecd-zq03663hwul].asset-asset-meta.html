HOW TO TAKE THE NATIONAL SCHOLARSHIP TEST (NST):

Below are the guidelines to be followed for the BBA/BMS NST (IPM BBA National Scholarship Test)
  • This test should be taken by IPM, BBA/BMS aspirants who wish to join IMS test prep program
  • The test link will be available on 24th,December-21 on the same page & it will be activated on 26th,December-21 at 10:00am
  • Last date to register for this scholarship test is 25th,December- 21 at 10:00 PM
  • Students can create a password of their choice at the time of registration. They are recommended to write & save it for future reference
  • Students will get the score card upon successful submission of the test. No detailed analytics will be provided for this test
  • Students can get scholarships up to 50% (Based on the score) on IPM, BBA/BMS programs offered by IMS
  • Students will receive the waiver codes through email by 29th December, 2021
Technical Instructions
  • Please ensure stable internet connectivity. The test will not be submitted if the internet connectivity is lost. In such a case, don’t close the browser window or refresh the browser till the internet connectivity is restored.
  • Don’t run the test in an incognito/private window.
  • Cookies ‘settings should be Allow local data to be set (recommended). This is the default setting. Don’t change this setting.
  • Test Machines should be free of viruses, spyware and malware.
