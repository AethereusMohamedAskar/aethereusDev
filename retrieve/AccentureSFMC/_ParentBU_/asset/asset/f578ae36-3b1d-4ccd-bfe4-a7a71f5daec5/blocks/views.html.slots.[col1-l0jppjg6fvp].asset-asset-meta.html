IMS, the pioneer in the test preparation industry, has been training and mentoring students for more than four decades for various entrance exams of postgraduate and undergraduate studies in India and abroad.

With 14 centers in Mumbai, no student is too far from an IMS center, each of which is located at a walking distance from major metro stations or bus stops.

All our centers are equipped with adequate classrooms, computer labs, and libraries. Students are encouraged to use classroom facilities for group-study and labs for taking online tests.

IMS philosophy takes us beyond teaching, and focuses on mentoring students to help achieve their career goals. Our mentors help students set their career aspirations, shortlist institutes/programs that meet their aspirations and continuously guide them on the path to achieve success.

Programs Offered

  • CLASSROOM PROGRAMS
  • LIVE ONLINE PROGRAMS

BRISK (3-months)

440 HRS program (Ideal for students who have just completed Class 12th)

Program Features

  • 170 hrs of concept classes for new CLAT pattern & other law exams
  • 80 hrs of concept videos for smart revision, recap videos for missed classes, online topic wise tests
  • 140 hrs of practice class videos on myIMS
  • 50 hrs of exam specific workshop videos on myIMS
  • 60+ mock tests covering CLAT & other important law exams

INTACT (1-year)

480 HRS program (Ideal for students who have entered Class 12th)

Program Features

  • 180 hours of concept classes & weekly GK sessions, syllabus completed before Class XII board exam break
  • 20 hrs of live, CLAT mock test solving sessions & early exposure to practice questions
  • Option to repeat classes with crash course after 12th board exams: March-April
  • 50 hrs of exam specific workshops after class XII boards
  • 140 hours of practice class videos on IMS live platform
  • 80 hours of concept videos, class recap videos, topic wise tests on myIMS

EXTENT (2-years)

520 HRS program (Ideal for students who have entered Class 11th)

Program Features

  • More flexibility due to sufficient spacing of the program
  • 180+ hours of concept classes & weekly GK sessions, syllabus completion before Class 11th exam (Yr 1)
  • 200 hours of practice of exam level questions & live mock test solving: June – Dec (Yr 2)
  • Option to repeat concept classes: June-Dec (in Yr 2)
  • 50 hours of exam specific workshops after class XII boards
  • 80 hours of concept videos, class recap videos, topic wise tests on myIMS

CLAT MAXIMISER (440 HRS)

Ideal for droppers who want to reappear in CLAT for rank/college improvement

Program Features

  • 170 hours of Need-based concept classes, module tests
  • 190 hrs of Practice classes & exam specific workshops
  • Additional practice@Home (Practice Module, past papers)
  • 60+ mock tests for CLAT & other exams
  • 80 hrs of concept videos for smart revision, recap videos for missed classes

1-yr, 2-yr and crash course options are available in online, live mode for students who do not have IMS centre near their residence or for those who wish to study from comfort of their home, without attending physical classes
