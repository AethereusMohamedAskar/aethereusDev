Personalization – Personalization 2.0

With every piece of Intelligence available to support business decisions, it's easy to forget that eventually, it's going to be a human who'll be making the final choice. For brands to be not just successful but meaningful over time, they will have to account for the combination of contexts (global x external x internal) that their consumers are in. They will also need to consider the rational and emotional decision factors and the evolving heuristics consumers use to make these decisions.
